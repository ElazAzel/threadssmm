# User Guide — Threads SMM Agent

## Getting Started

1. Go to the landing page and click **Начать бесплатно**
2. Complete the onboarding wizard (5 steps):
   - Name your workspace
   - Describe your brand (niche, audience, tone)
   - Set your timezone and region
   - Connect or add your Threads profile
   - Review and finish
3. Invite team members from the **Команда** page

## AI Studio

Create content with AI assistance:

1. Go to **Студия**
2. Enter the main idea/topic
3. Click **Создать варианты** — 3 variants are generated
4. Review scores: hook score + compliance score
5. Select your preferred variant
6. Click **Согласовать** to send for approval
7. After approval, schedule or publish

## Content Calendar

- Switch between **Месяц** and **Список** views
- Drag to reschedule
- Click a post to edit or publish immediately

## Approvals

- Review pending drafts
- Approve, reject, or request changes
- See compliance notes and risk scores

## RSS Monitoring

- Add RSS sources to monitor competitors and trends
- Each item shows relevance score, sentiment, and AI recommendation
- Reply directly or create a new post based on monitoring insights

## Analytics

Track post performance:
- Views, likes, replies, reposts, quotes
- Follower growth
- Export data as CSV

## Account Management

- Connect via Meta OAuth (recommended)
- Or add manually with just a username
- Assign accounts to brands
- Monitor token expiration and reconnect when needed

## Settings

Configure workspace-level policies:
- **Security**: enable/disable, strictness level
- **AI**: credit limits, provider settings
- **Notifications**: email/in-app preferences
- **Audit log**: view all workspace actions

## Billing

- Free tier: 50 AI credits/month
- Pro: 500 credits, team collaboration, advanced analytics
- Enterprise: custom limits, priority support
